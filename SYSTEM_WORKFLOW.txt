1. TECHNOLOGY & PRODUCT STACK
---------------------------------------------------------
CORE ENGINE:
- Frontend: React 19 (Vite) - Ultra-fast, modern UI experience.
- Authentication: Firebase Auth - Enterprise-grade security for Google, Email, and Phone OTP.
- Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) - Persistent, real-time data storage with millisecond sync.
- Styling: Vanilla CSS + Tailwind - Premium, custom-crafted aesthetics.

ADVANTAGES OF OUR SYSTEM:
- Hybrid Security: Combines Firebase's world-class identity management with Supabase's powerful data rules.
- Real-Time Journey: Customers see their order progress "live" without ever refreshing the page.
- Premium Aesthetics: High-end "Service Journey" timelines and vertical trackers that wow users.
- Business Ready: Includes professional Tax Invoice/Bill generation and itemized tracking.
- Zero Data Loss: 100% permanent storage on cloud servers—business data never "starts from zero."

2. THE "PREMIUM SERVICE JOURNEY" (8-STAGE WORKFLOW)
---------------------------------------------------------
Every order follows a meticulously tracked 8-stage process:
1. Order Confirmed: Customer booking is received and validated.
2. Picked Up: Clothes are collected from the customer's doorstep.
3. In Cleaning: Items are in the washing, dry-cleaning, or sorting phase.
4. Final Finishing: Professional pressing, ironing, and folding.
5. Final Inspection: Expert quality check to ensure perfection.
6. Ready for Dispatch: Packed, invoiced, and waiting for delivery.
7. Out for Delivery: The driver is on the way to the customer.
8. Completed & Delivered: Final handoff and transaction complete.

3. CUSTOMER WORKFLOW
---------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: SECURE AUTHENTICATION
- Customer signs up via Google (Instant), Phone (OTP), or Email.
- Identity is verified via Firebase; a matching profile is created in Supabase.

STEP 2: EASY BOOKING
- User selects services (Wash, Iron, Dry Clean), quantity, and fabric.
- Real-time price calculation ensures transparency.
- Pickup address and time-window are scheduled.

STEP 3: LIVE TRACKING (The "Journey")
- Customer sees a beautiful vertical timeline on their "My Account" page.
- Current stage pulses with a live indicator.
- Completed stages show exact time-stamps of processing.

STEP 4: SELF-SERVICE INVOICING
- Customer can print their own professional Tax Invoice directly from their account.

4. ADMIN / POS WORKFLOW
---------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: REAL-TIME DASHBOARD
- Admin sees "Live" stats: Today's Revenue, Active Orders, and New Customers.
- New orders pop up instantly without page reloads.

STEP 2: ORDER MANAGEMENT
- Admin opens an order to see full itemized lists and customer contact details.
- "Advance Journey" button: Moves the order through the 8 stages with one click.
- "Record Payment": Handles Cash, Card, or UPI transactions securely.

STEP 3: BUSINESS OPERATIONS
- Print Professional Bill: Generates high-end receipts with business branding.
- Customer CRM: Full history and profile management for every client.
- Global Settings: Control business name, currency, and service tax rates.

5. DATA & SECURITY INFRASTRUCTURE
---------------------------------------------------------
- Environment Variables (.env): All API keys are hidden in a private file.
- .env.example: A safe template for project sharing without exposing secrets.
- Real-time Replication: Enabled for 'orders', 'customers', and 'logs' tables.
- CHECK Constraints: Database-level rules ensure only valid statuses are saved.
- Optimistic UI: The app updates instantly on click, then saves to DB in the background.
